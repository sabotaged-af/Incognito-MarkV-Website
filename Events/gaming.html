General Guidelines


    Rules


  • All participants need to join the discord server for live-communication purposes.

  • Any form of cheating would straight away lead to the disqualification of the team.

  • All players will be monitored from the server as well as individually spectated.

  • Each team would be registered in their leader's name.

  • Please give the following information:

    • Name (team representative)

    • Discord id

    • School

    • Team name

  • Players should have a strong and consistent internet connection. Typical gamer excuses like lag will not be considered.Since INCOGNITO is an online event, participants will use their own equipment. We will NOT be responsible in for laggy software or hardware in any way.

  • if a team doesn’t join on the allotted time, they wouldn’t be given another chance.

  • Please show sportsmanship do NOT argue with the MODS , THEIR decision is FINAL

BRAWLHALLA - Qualifiers


  • Brawlhalla(4v4) 4 players from each team will play against the team in the bracket which will be formed 2 days prior game day.

  • The winning teams continue in the bracket and the losing team will be eliminated.

  • Top 10-15 teams will qualify for valorant.

  • A player from both teams should stay in discord call with the moderator during the complete match.

VALORANT - Finals


  • Participants must have a good internet and have VALORANT pre-installed no excuses will be entertained.

  • The map and attacking/defending side would be decided by toss done on call by the moderator alongside both team captain.

  • The whole game would be spectated by the moderators.

  • There will be no second chance for the eliminated teams.

  • Top 4 teams will have playoffs and on that basis 1st,2nd,3rd position will be decided.
