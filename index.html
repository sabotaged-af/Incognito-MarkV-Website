About Incognito


Incognito 2021, is the latest iteration of the annual Tech-Fest organised by Somerville School, Noida

Driven by the vision to provide the students a platform to showcase their excellence in the field of Computer Science and Technology, Incognito aims to ignite the spark of passion and set ablaze the fire of imagination in the minds of the students.
